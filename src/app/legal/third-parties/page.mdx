# Third-Party Services

_Last updated: July 20, 2025_

We use a number of trusted third-party services to provide and enhance the functionality, performance, security, and analytics of our website. Below is a list of these services, along with their purpose and links to their respective privacy policies.

---

## Analytics & Tracking

### **Google Analytics**

Used to collect aggregated information about user behavior and site performance.  
[Google Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)

### **Hotjar**

Provides heatmaps, session recordings, and user feedback tools to improve usability.  
[Hotjar Privacy Policy](https://www.hotjar.com/legal/policies/privacy/)

---

## Advertising & Marketing

### **Google Ads / DoubleClick**

Used for remarketing and serving personalized ads across the web.  
[Google Ads Policy](https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads)

### **Meta Pixel (Facebook/Instagram)**

Tracks interactions to measure ad performance and build custom audiences.  
[Meta Privacy Policy](https://www.facebook.com/policy.php)

---

## Content Delivery & Hosting

### **Cloudflare**

Provides CDN, DDoS protection, and performance optimization.  
[Cloudflare Privacy Policy](https://www.cloudflare.com/privacypolicy/)

### **Vercel / Netlify / AWS / etc.**

(Adjust to what you use) Used to host and deploy the website.  
[Vercel Privacy Policy](https://vercel.com/legal/privacy-policy)

---

## Embedded Media

### **YouTube**

Videos embedded from YouTube may collect viewer interaction data.  
[YouTube Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)

### **Vimeo**

Used to embed and stream high-quality videos.  
[Vimeo Privacy Policy](https://vimeo.com/privacy)

---

## Communication Tools

### **Mailchimp / Brevo / etc.**

Used to manage newsletters, transactional emails, and other communications.  
[Mailchimp Privacy Policy](https://mailchimp.com/legal/privacy/)

### **reCAPTCHA (Google)**

Used to prevent spam and abuse when submitting forms.  
[reCAPTCHA Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)

---

## Developer Tools

### **Sentry**

Monitors and logs errors and performance issues in real time.  
[Sentry Privacy Policy](https://sentry.io/privacy/)

### **LogRocket / PostHog (optional)**

Records sessions and provides product analytics.  
[LogRocket Privacy](https://logrocket.com/privacy)

---

## Notes

We carefully evaluate the data practices of third-party providers before integrating them and only use services that comply with relevant privacy and data protection regulations such as GDPR or CCPA.
