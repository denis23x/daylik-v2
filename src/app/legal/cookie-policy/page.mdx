---

## 1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device (computer, smartphone, etc.) when you visit websites. They help websites remember your actions and preferences over time.

---

## 2. Types of Cookies We Use

### a. Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for the website to function and cannot be switched off. They are usually set in response to your actions, such as logging in or filling out forms.

### b. Performance Cookies

These cookies collect information about how visitors use our website (e.g. most visited pages, error messages). All information is anonymous and used to improve site performance.

### c. Functional Cookies

These cookies allow the website to remember choices you make (like your language or region) to provide enhanced functionality and personalization.

### d. Targeting / Advertising Cookies

These cookies may be set through our site by our advertising partners to build a profile of your interests and show you relevant ads on other sites.

---

## 3. How to Manage Cookies

You can control or delete cookies as you wish. Most browsers allow you to refuse or delete cookies through their settings. However, disabling cookies may affect your experience on our website.

Learn how to manage cookies in:

- [Google Chrome](https://support.google.com/chrome/answer/95647)
- [Mozilla Firefox](https://support.mozilla.org/en-US/kb/enable-and-disable-cookies)
- [Safari](https://support.apple.com/en-us/HT201265)
- [Microsoft Edge](https://support.microsoft.com/en-us/help/4027947)

---

## 4. Third-Party Cookies

We may also use third-party services (like Google Analytics) that place cookies to collect data for analytics or advertising purposes. We do not control these cookies. Please check the third parties' privacy policies.
